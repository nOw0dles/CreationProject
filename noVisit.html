You reject their invitation, wanting to go home as soon as possible. You find a nice inn to stay at for the night. While you peacefully enjoy your supper, the King and Queen burst through the doors and make a beeline straight towards you. They beg you to tell stories of your adventures. Seeing as they won't leave until you tell them a story, you reluctantly agree to tell a single story. Pick a story to tell.
